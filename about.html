About

Makerinchief offers kits and resources designed to level up your skills with electronics, programming and fabrication while keeping things fun with projects you can reuse or modify to create something new. All kits are designed to be built using 3D printers, laser cutters, hot glue guns and maybe some tape. Most of these can be found at your local makerspace!

For a fun tool to find a local makerspace in your state, have a look at the link below:

findMakerspace()
